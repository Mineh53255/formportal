Important Scholarship Information

Scholarships are awarded on the basis of need, academic performance, and commitment to education. GPFA has limited resources and cannot provide assistance to all applicants. Do not request more assistance than you need.

If you are awarded a scholarship from another organization, you must inform GPFA immediately.

Important Instructions
  • Complete this application with your parent or guardian
  • Include supporting documentation (bank statements, pay slips, loan documents)
  • Scholarships are only for students who cannot afford full fees
  • Include a letter detailing reasons why you need a scholarship
  • Do not request more assistance than you need - resources are limited
  • All information must be accurate. Falsification results in termination of award
